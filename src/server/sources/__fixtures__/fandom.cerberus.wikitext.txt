The '''Cerberus''' is a limited legendary [[Pets|pet]] in ''Adopt Me!'' that was released on October 28, 2020, as part of the [[Halloween Event (2020)]]. It cost {{Robux|500}} and was on sale for {{Robux|400}} in the [[Final Stop Shop]]. As it has left the game on May 18, 2023, it can now only be obtained through [[Trade System|trading]].

The Cerberus could also have been purchased from the Shop icon on the top right of a player's screen and was previously located in the [[Pet Shop]].

== Appearance ==
The Cerberus is a three-headed dark gray hound. Parts of its heads, along with its eyes, ears, noses, and paws, are colored black, with the exception of a light gray underbelly. Each of the three heads have a pink tongue sticking out, two ears, two black eyes, a black nose, and gray head fur.    

== Tricks ==
Listed below are the tricks the Cerberus learns in order:
*Newborn - Sit
*Junior - Lay Down
*Pre-Teen - Beg
*Teen - Jump
*Post-Teen - Trick 1
*Full Grown - Trick 2

== Neon Appearance ==
The [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Cerberus glows bright lavender on the insides of its ears, the fur on its chest, and its tail.

== Mega Neon Appearance ==
The [[Mega Neons|Mega Neon]] Cerberus glows in the same areas as the [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Cerberus, except those areas cycle through the colors of the rainbow.

== Trivia ==
*The ''Adopt Me!'' Cerberus is modelled after the mythological dog [[wikipedia:Cerberus|Cerberus]].
*Despite being introduced during the [[Halloween Event (2020)]], it remained in ''Adopt Me!'' as a [[Gamepasses|gamepass]] [[Pets|pet]] and was moved to the [[Pet Shop]].
