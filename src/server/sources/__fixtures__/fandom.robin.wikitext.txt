The '''Robin''' is a limited common [[Pets|pet]] in ''Adopt Me!'' that was released on December 14, 2019, as a part of the [[Christmas Event (2019)]]. As it is now unavailable, it can only be obtained by [[Trade System|trading]] or by hatching any remaining [[Christmas Egg]]s.

Players have a 45% chance of hatching a Robin from the [[Christmas Egg]].

== Appearance ==
The Robin has a dark orange underbelly and part of the face, and the outer area of its body is brown. It has a slightly lighter orange for its legs, black beady eyes, a black beak, and is complete with three tail feathers.

== Tricks ==
Here are the tricks the Robin learns in order:
*Newborn - Sit
*Junior - Lay Down
*Pre-Teen - Bounce 
*Teen - Joy
*Post-Teen - Dance 1
*Full Grown - Dance 2

== Neon Appearance ==
The [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Robin glows orange-yellow on the inside of its body, and a slightly darker version of that same color on its feet.

== Mega Neon Appearance ==
The [[Mega Neons|Mega Neon]] Robin glows in the same areas as the [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Robin, except those areas cycle through the colors of the rainbow.
