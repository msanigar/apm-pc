The '''Bat Dragon''' is a limited legendary [[Pets|pet]] in ''Adopt Me!'' that was released during the [[Halloween Event (2019)]], along with the [[Evil Unicorn]]. It could have been purchased for {{Candy|180,000}} in the Candy Trading Shop.  Since its event has ended, the Bat Dragon is now only obtainable through [[Trade System|trading]].

== Appearance ==
The Bat Dragon has a light brown body, two small orange horns on its head, two beady black eyes, black bat-like ears with a light brownish color inside, an orange nose on its snout, black and red curved wings, and two small fangs protruding from its mouth. Its underbelly is a light tan color, with the tip of its tail being black with an orange tip that is pointed. It has orange tail spikes on the back of its body.

== Tricks ==
Here are the tricks the Bat Dragon learns in order:
*Newborn - Sit
*Junior - Lay Down
*Pre-Teen - Joy
*Teen - Jump
*Post-Teen - Twirl Around 
*Full Grown - Triple Flip

== Neon Appearance ==
The [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Bat Dragon's wings and the tip of its tail glow in a deep neon orange color, while its horns, nose, and the spikes on its tail glow in a light orange color.

== Mega Neon Appearance ==
The [[Mega Neons|Mega Neon]] Bat Dragon glows in the same areas as the [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Bat Dragon, except those areas cycle through white, magenta, lavender, and turquoise.

== Trivia ==
*The Bat Dragon has three reskins known as the [[Strawberry Shortcake Bat Dragon]], [[Chocolate Chip Bat Dragon]], and [[Fairy Bat Dragon]].
