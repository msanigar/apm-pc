The '''RGB Reward Box''' is a legendary [[Gifts|gift]] in ''Adopt Me!'' that was released during the [[Task Board]] update on January 27, 2022. It can be obtained by completing tasks in the "Pets" section in the [[Guide]]; up to five RGB Reward Boxes are obtainable per account. It can also be obtained as a reward for aging up [[pets]], or through [[Trade System|trading]].

Before November 14, 2025, it could have obtained by completing a task marked with a "flame" icon on the [[Task Board]], which required players to make a [[Neon Pets|Neon]] or [[Mega Neons|Mega Neon]] [[Pets|pet]] as their task, which rewarded one and three RGB Reward Boxes respectively once completed.

Players have a 44% chance to get a common item, a 30% chance to get an uncommon item, a 14.5% chance to get a rare item, a 7% chance to get an ultra-rare [[Vehicles|vehicle]], and a 4.5% chance to get a legendary [[Pets|pet]] from an RGB Reward Box.

== Appearance ==
The RGB Reward Box features a rainbow box with with a thick black rim and handles. On the front of the box, players can see a multicolored paw.
