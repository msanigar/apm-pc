The '''Sasquatch''' is a limited rare [[Pets|pet]] in ''Adopt Me!'' that was released on August 19, 2021. It can be obtained by hatching a [[Mythic Egg]]. Since the [[Mythic Egg]] left the game on March 17, 2022, the Sasquatch is now only obtainable through [[Trade System|trading]] or by hatching any remaining [[Mythic Egg]]s.

Players have a 34% chance of hatching a rare [[Pets|pet]] from the [[Mythic Egg]], but only a 17% chance of hatching the Sasquatch.

== Appearance ==
The Sasquatch has a spherical brown body with layers of brown fur, with a beige underside. It features two beige arms with three dark brown claws, and two large, dark brown feet. It has a beige head with two beady black eyes, a brown nose, and dark brown hair that covers most of its head, as well as a tuft of brown hair on top.

== Tricks ==
Listed below are the tricks the Sasquatch learns in order:
*Newborn - Sit
*Junior - Joyful
*Pre-Teen - Beg
*Teen - Jump
*Post-Teen - Trick 1
*Full Grown - Trick 2

== Neon Appearance ==
The [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Sasquatch glows bright orange around its arms and on the fur around its head.

== Mega Neon Appearance ==
The [[Mega Neons|Mega Neon]] Sasquatch glows in the same areas as the [[Neon Pets|Neon]] Sasquatch, except those areas cycle through the colors of the rainbow.

== Trivia ==
*The [[Yeti]] features a similar body shape and arms to the Sasquatch.
*A furniture item known as the "Camping with Friends" (now known as the "Camping with Sasquatch") shows this pet despite this furniture item being released before the pet.
